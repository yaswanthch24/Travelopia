There are many pagan sites to visit as well as UNESCO World Heritage-listed churches, which are — almost without exception — built in stunning locations. Surrounded by forests, perched on the top of dramatic gorges or silhouetted before snow-capped mountains, they draw us to magnificent open spaces filled with a huge variety of trees and wild flowers.
